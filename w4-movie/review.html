REVIEW

Gabriel Posillo August 15th 2021


Where can I even begin in an attempt to explain why I love this film so much? This film has so much depth! Watchmen begins by begging the question "What if we had allowed costumed vigilantes to fight crime by night at their own risk?" The movie then goes on to further experiment with different what-if's, such as the notion of Richard Nixon serving three consecutive terms, thus perpetuating the Cold War. The Watchmen themselves are not portrayed as gaudy, shallow superheroes, they are complex people with their own complex problems. This makes the Watchmen highly relatable. This movie is so detailed that I still catch something new every time I watch it. My review cannot do this film justice, go watch it!

TRIVIA:

  • Jackie Earle Haley was a major fan of the comic book. He actively campaigned for the part of Rorschach.
  • If you look at all of the American flags in the film they have 51 stars. In this alternate universe Vietnam became the 51st state after surrendering to Dr. Manhattan
  • The original trailer premiered before 2008's The Dark Knight. The trailer sparked such interest that the comic shot back to the top of the bestseller list.
